Bread and Roses is an online publication that explores the various and ever-changing relationships between art and labor. Labor is often thought of as work—especially physical work—but political theorists and artists have tried to expand the definition into something more deep and meaningful. Throughout history, the concept of labor has evolved in response to the social and technological advancements that have changed existing modes of production.

Anna Feng and Amanda Grossman
Spring 2018
